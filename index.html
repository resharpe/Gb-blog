35 years gone
she appears from nowhere in my dream
as defeated as she always was
as ancient and marked and beautiful.
She smiles unmoving when I touch her cheek.

I sit rewriting the same song
I have written for 35 years
as she comes to look
pressing her side to mine
across a dry border.

But the song is buried
the words opaque
and I will not tell her
I have done much better things.
